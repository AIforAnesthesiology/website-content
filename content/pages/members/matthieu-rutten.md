Matthieu Rutten is Associate Professor in Translational Clinical Imaging in the Diagnostic Image Analysis Group at Radboud University Medical Center. He is dedicated musculoskeletal, breast and interventional radiologist. He graduated at Radboud University Nijmegen in 1989, completed his radiology residency at Radboud University Medical Center in 1996 and his PhD at the Radboud University Nijmegen on Ultrasound of the Shoulder - Efficacy studies in 2010. He has (co-)authored over 100 publications in (inter)national journals.
