Koen Dercksen is a PhD candidate at the Radboud University, working in the AI
for Health lab. He studied Data Science at the Radboud University, obtaining
his Master's degree in 2019. At AI for Health, he works on improving patients'
understanding of electronic health records by building systems that can provide
additional information, explanation and laymen translation of medical text.
He is supervised by [Arjen P. de Vries](https://www.ru.nl/english/people/vries-a-de/),
[Faegheh Hasibi](http://hasibi.com/), and [member/bram-van-ginneken].
