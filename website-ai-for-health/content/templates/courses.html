AI for Health course

AI is playing an increasingly important role in healthcare. It offers healtcare professionals great opportunities to improve the care they provide, but it can be hard to get a good overview of the field and the applications AI has.
Therefore Radboud AI for Health together with the Jheronimus Academy of Data Science (JADS) in Den Bosch runs two courses every year for a group of around 25 professionals from Radboudumc interested to learn more about AI applications in healthcare. The course program consists of lectures and practical exercises, and concludes with a practical project around a Radboudumc case. Why follow this course?
  • AI is changing healthcare rapidly, this course gives you the opportunity to take a leading role in this process<\li>
  • Improve patient care by correclty deploying AI applications<\li>
  • Take away misconceptions about AI and learn what is really entails<\li>
