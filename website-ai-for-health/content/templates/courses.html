AI for Health course

Radboud AI for Health plans to run two courses every year for a group of around 25 professionals from Radboudumc interested to learn more about AI applications in healthcare. The course program will consist of lectures and practical exercises, and will conclude with a practical project around a Radboudumc case. The course is a collaboration between Radboudumc and the Jheronimus Academy of Data Science (JADS) in Den Bosch.
